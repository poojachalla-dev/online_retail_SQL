This dashboard provides an interactive visualization of the **Online Retail dataset**, helping analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends across different countries.

The goal of this dashboard is to present **key business insights** through clear and interactive visualizations.

---

## 📌 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard highlights the following key metrics and visualizations:

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue** – Overall revenue generated from all transactions.
* **Total Customers** – Number of unique customers in the dataset.
* **Total Orders** – Total number of invoices/orders processed.

These KPIs provide a quick summary of the business performance.

---

### 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend

A **line chart** showing how revenue changes over time.

**Purpose:**

* Identify sales growth or decline
* Detect seasonal patterns
* Monitor revenue performance monthly

---

### 🌍 Revenue by Country

A **bar chart** comparing revenue generated from different countries.

**Purpose:**

* Identify top-performing markets
* Understand geographic distribution of sales
* Support market expansion decisions

---

### 🛍️ Top 10 Products by Revenue

A **horizontal bar chart** displaying the products that generate the highest revenue.

**Purpose:**

* Identify best-selling products
* Understand product demand
* Support inventory and pricing strategies

---

### 👥 Top Customers by Revenue

A chart highlighting customers contributing the most revenue.

**Purpose:**

* Identify high-value customers
* Support customer relationship strategies
* Analyze purchasing behavior

---

## 🎛️ Dashboard Interactivity

The dashboard includes interactive features that allow users to explore the data:

* **Country Filter** – View sales data for a specific country
* **Revenue Filter** – Analyze purchases from customers
* **Description Filter** – Focus on specific products

These features help users perform deeper analysis directly from the dashboard.

---

## 📊 Business Insights

From this dashboard, analysts can easily identify:

* Countries generating the highest revenue
* Products driving the majority of sales
* High-value customers
* Monthly sales trends and seasonal patterns

This enables **data-driven business decisions**.
